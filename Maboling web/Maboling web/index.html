MABOLING

a food truck that goes around all over Jakarta and sells various kinds of rice bowls.

Starting from 4 friends who have an idea to travel around Jakarta while making money. So they come with the idea of a food truck that sells rice bowls that are practical to take anywhere and fast to serve. First ride from 2022 and still traveling until now.
